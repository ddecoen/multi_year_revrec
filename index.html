Term-Based vs Perpetual License Example

Term-Based Software (Electronic Delivery - Like Coder)

  • License: Term-based but delivered electronically
  • Recognition: License at point in time, support over time
  • Rationale: Customer gains control when electronically delivered
  • Pattern: License in Year 1, support ratably over term

Perpetual Software (Traditional)

  • License: Indefinite right to use software
  • Recognition: License at point in time, support over time
  • Rationale: Customer gains control immediately
  • Pattern: Front-loaded recognition in Year 1
